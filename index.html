Who We Are
"Not just a grant writing shop...a grant thought partner."

Pairing Big-4 discipline with practical execution to turn ideas into funded outcomes.

GRANTED is a full-spectrum grant solutions firm and thought partner to mission-driven organizations across the US. From the first discovery conversation through final closeout, we find the grants, build the strategy, write the proposals, and manage the compliance. Your team stays focused on the work that matters most.

Find Funding
Stop chasing grants that don't fit. We identify the right opportunities, score them against your mission, and tell you exactly which ones are worth pursuing.
Win Awards
Reviewers score what they see. We build applications around what wins: narrative, budget, and strategy aligned to the criteria that matter.
Manage Impact
Winning is the beginning. We keep your award on track through every reporting deadline, compliance requirement, and closeout milestone.
How We Start
"Every engagement starts with a conversation."

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Before we recommend anything, we do a discovery call — a working conversation to understand your organization, your goals, and which level of support actually makes sense for you.
