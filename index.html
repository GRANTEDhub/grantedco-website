Who We Are
"We are not a grant-writing shop — we are a strategic partner."

Pairing Big-4 discipline with practical execution to turn ideas into funded outcomes.

GRANTED is a full-spectrum grant solutions firm and thought partner to local governments, tribal nations, nonprofits, and institutions nationwide. From the first discovery conversation through final closeout, we build the strategy, write the proposals, and manage the compliance — so your team stays focused on the work that matters most.
