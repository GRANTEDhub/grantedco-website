What We Do

We're not a grant-writing shop. We're a strategic partner.

GRANTED delivers full-spectrum grant support — from the first opportunity scan through final closeout. We build strategy, write proposals, and manage compliance so your team can focus on the work that matters.

View all services →
01
Grant Research
We scan the full funding landscape — federal, state, and private — and deliver a scored, prioritized roadmap of opportunities matched to your specific eligibility and mission. No generic lists.
02
Grant Writing
Custom narratives, budgets, workplans, and attachments — built around what reviewers actually score. We coordinate partners, pull the data, and handle compliance review before submission.
03
Strategic Advisory
Portfolio design, pipeline governance, match strategy, and partner mapping. We help leadership build a funding strategy with predictable execution — not a reactive scramble.
04
Grant Administration
Post-award compliance end-to-end: kickoffs, procurement, subrecipient oversight, reporting, drawdowns, change requests, audit readiness, and closeout. Nothing falls through the cracks.
