Who We Are
Not a Writing Shop.
A Strategic Partner.

GRANTED is a full-spectrum grant solutions firm and thought partner to local governments, tribal nations, nonprofits, and institutions nationwide. We are not a grant-writing shop — we are a strategic partner, pairing Big-4 discipline with practical execution to turn ideas into funded outcomes. From the first discovery conversation through final closeout, we build the strategy, write the proposals, and manage the compliance — so your team stays focused on the work that matters most.
