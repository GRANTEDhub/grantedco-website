What We Do

Our Services

From opportunity identification through post-award closeout — we build the strategy, write the proposals, and manage the compliance.

Service Areas
Full-Spectrum Grant Solutions
01
Find Funding
Grant Research

Our GRANTED Roadmap delivers a tailored list of high-impact grants mapped to your eligible projects and priorities. We build a forecast and calendar, then monitor continuously so you never miss a relevant opportunity.

  • Eligibility analysis and fit scoring
  • Active and forecasted opportunity identification
  • Deadline calendar and monitoring alerts
  • Federal, state, and private sector coverage
02
Win Awards
Grant Writing

Custom quotes matched to opportunity complexity. We deliver funder-aligned narratives, budgets, workplans, and attachments — coordinating partners and data for a compliant, competitive submission.

  • Narrative development and funder alignment
  • Budget and workplan preparation
  • Partner coordination and letters of support
  • Compliance review before submission
03
Build Capacity
Strategic Advisory

We shape funding strategy and long-term capacity: portfolio design, pipeline governance, partner mapping, match and in-kind strategy, and decision frameworks — so leadership gains clarity and predictable execution.

  • Funding portfolio design
  • Match and in-kind strategy
  • Partner identification and mapping
  • Pipeline governance and reporting
04
Manage Impact
Grant Administration

Post-award, we manage compliance end-to-end: kickoff, procurement, subrecipient oversight, documentation, reporting, drawdowns, change requests, audit readiness, and closeout.

  • Kickoff and subrecipient oversight
  • Reporting and drawdown management
  • Audit readiness and documentation
  • Closeout and final reporting
