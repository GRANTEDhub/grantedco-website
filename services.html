What We Do

Our Services

From opportunity identification through post-award closeout: we build the strategy, write the proposals, and manage the compliance.

Service Areas
Full-Spectrum Grant Solutions
01
Find Funding
Grant Research

We build your GRANTED Roadmap: a scored, prioritized list of high-impact opportunities mapped to your eligible projects. We forecast the pipeline, set the calendar, and monitor continuously so nothing worth pursuing slips by.

  • Eligibility analysis and fit scoring
  • Active and forecasted opportunity identification
  • Deadline calendar and real-time monitoring alerts
  • Federal, state, and private sector coverage
02
Win Awards
Grant Writing

Every application is scoped to the opportunity. We write funder-aligned narratives, build compliant budgets, and coordinate everything reviewers need to score you at the top.

  • Narrative development and funder alignment
  • Budget and workplan preparation
  • Partner coordination and letters of support
  • Compliance review before submission
03
Build Capacity
Strategic Advisory

We shape your funding strategy from the top: portfolio design, pipeline governance, partner mapping, match planning, and decision frameworks that give leadership clarity and predictable execution.

  • Funding portfolio design
  • Match and in-kind strategy
  • Partner identification and mapping
  • Pipeline governance and reporting
04
Manage Impact
Grant Administration

Winning is the beginning. We manage post-award compliance end-to-end so your award performs, your audits are clean, and your closeout is airtight.

  • Kickoff and subrecipient oversight
  • Reporting and drawdown management
  • Audit readiness and documentation
  • Closeout and final reporting
