About GRANTED

GRANTED is a tech-enabled grant advisory firm based in Bentonville, Arkansas. We help nonprofits, local governments, higher education, and small businesses find, win, and manage grant funding — with over $100 million in non-dilutive funding secured for our clients.

We're a small, founder-led team at a pivotal stage of growth, transitioning to an AI-native platform to scale our mission nationally. The founding team has built companies before, knows what it doesn't know, and is genuinely invested in the growth of people around them. You won't be managed into a box here.

The Role

You'll work alongside the founder across platform development, grant research and writing, business development, government relations, marketing, project management, client relationships, legal coordination, and more. Some of it you'll know. Most of it you'll figure out. All of it will matter from day one.

What We're Looking For

Education and experience aren't always reliable hiring metrics — especially in a startup. What we need is someone who can adapt quickly, contribute across shifting areas of the business, and bring real capability from day one.

Three things are required:

  • Bachelor's degree
  • Demonstrated fluency with AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, or equivalent)
  • Located in Northwest Arkansas

We'd love to see experience in grant writing or research, go-to-market strategy, startup environments, vibe coding or low-code automation, and operations. But none of it is a dealbreaker. If you've held a role that required you to context-switch constantly, operate across multiple disciplines, and figure things out on the fly — tell us about it.

How to Apply

Use the Apply button in the sidebar. We take cover letters seriously. Skip the template — tell us your story, why this role, and why you're the right person for it. A great note will do more than any resume.

Ready to Apply?

Upload your resume. Strong applicants receive a short video interview to answer on your own time. No scheduling required for round one.
