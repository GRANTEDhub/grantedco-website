Our Origin
Built from the Ground Up

In 2011, a tornado leveled Joplin, Missouri — the hometown of our founder, Shannon Anastosopolos. In the years that followed, Shannon joined Deloitte and later PwC, helping administer the city's recovery through some of the most complex federal grant programs in the country. Rebuilding housing. Rebuilding infrastructure. Rebuilding a community from the ground up.

That experience revealed something that hasn't changed: organizations with the right projects and the right mission consistently lose ground to those with better grant infrastructure. GRANTED was built to fix that.

Based in Northwest Arkansas, we bring Big-4 discipline to communities that shouldn't need a consulting firm to access public funding. We scout opportunities, write competitive proposals, and manage compliance end-to-end — so our clients can focus on the work, not the paperwork.

Founder & CEO
Shannon Anastosopolos
Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Shannon built GRANTED on a simple conviction: organizations with the right mission shouldn't lose to organizations with better grant infrastructure.

Before founding GRANTED, Shannon spent years at Deloitte and PwC administering some of the largest federal recovery programs in the country; managing over $10 billion in grant portfolios across local governments, tribal nations, and mission-driven organizations nationwide. That experience shaped a practice built on Big-4 rigor, not guesswork.

Today Shannon leads a firm that operates across the full grant lifecycle: from opportunity identification and proposal development to post-award compliance and closeout. His clients include local governments, nonprofits, health systems, small businesses, development organizations, and institutions navigating federal, state, and private funding at every scale.
