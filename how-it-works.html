A Clear Funding Strategy in Three Steps

For a single flat fee, we learn your organization, research the funding landscape, and deliver a prioritized roadmap of the grants you're best positioned to win, capped off with a live strategy session. No retainer, no commitment required to start.

Get Started
01
Discovery Call

A working session to understand your mission, priorities, projects, and capacity: the foundation for everything we research.

02
The Grant Report

A custom GRANTED Roadmap: a prioritized list of active and forecasted grants you're eligible for, with funder, deadline, award range, and fit.

03
Debrief & Strategy

A 1-hour strategy session to walk through the roadmap, answer questions, and map out your next moves and priorities.

If You'd Like to Keep Going
Ongoing Engagement Options

Navigate stands on its own, but most clients want a partner to help execute. Choose from two levels of ongoing engagement:

Build

12 months of daily grant monitoring and live alerts when relevant opportunities drop. You'll never miss a deadline that fits your mission.

Partner

A fractional grants team embedded in your organization all year: proposal development, consortium strategy, and priority access. A real grants function without the overhead of a full-time hire.
